What would you say if we don't check your servers (OCR, archive if applicable) and OCR quality and you or your own IT takes responsibility?
6 We would like to have a monitoring tool that reacts preventively to malfunctions so that there are no delays or major outages.

*The monitoring tool monitors the complete OCR server (and archive server, if applicable).		 What would you say if your complete processing route up to SAP is monitored by a monitoring tool and immediately raises an alarm when a fault occurs (e.g. service fails, faulty mail, etc.) ?
What would you say if your complete processing line is not monitored automatically and you have to detect and clean up errors manually?
7 If a monitoring tool is used, we would like Henrichsen Service to deal with the error messages directly and immediately in order to guarantee a smooth process. What would you say if you use a monitoring tool and the error messages are directly and immediately corrected by the Henrichsen service team?
What would you say if you use a monitoring tool and the error messages are not resolved by the Henrichsen service team, but you have to receive and process the error messages yourself?
8 We would like to have personal and individual support from our own service supervisor, whom we can contact directly in urgent cases and who will give us tips and help us with questions at regular intervals. What would you say if you received personal and customized support from your own service manager, whom you could contact directly in urgent cases and who would help you with questions and give you lots of tips in regular meetings?
What would you say if you don't receive personal support, regular meetings with tips and help, and you can only communicate with us via the ticket portal?
9 We would like to hand over tasks that take place on the OCR server such as "Check services and processes and activate if necessary" or "Diagnose log files and status of documents on the OCR server and clean up if necessary" to Henrichsen Support. What would you say if you do not perform tasks that take place on your OCR server, such as "Check services / processes and activate if necessary" or "Check log files and status of documents on the OCR server and tidy them up" yourself, but instead Henrichsen Support takes care of it?
What would you say if necessary tasks that take place on your OCR server are not handled by Henrichsen Support and you (or your IT) have to perform this activity independently and on a regular basis?
10 We would like to hand over 1st level support to Henrichsen Support, as we either do not have our own admins or would like to relieve our admins. 1st level tasks are e.g. clean up XX records, maintain users, clean up role resolution errors etc. What would you say if Henrichsen Support takes over the complete 1st level (tidy up XX records, create and maintain users, fix role resolution errors etc.) to relieve your own xSuite administrators?
What would you say if Hernichsen Support doesn't do 1st level and you do simple tasks like reordering XX records, creating and maintaining users, fixing role resolution errors, etc. yourself?

What would you say if your users turn first to your trained and defined key users in case of malfunctions and cannot contact Henrichsen Support directly?

What would you say if you can't customize your xSuite layout (new fields, columns, variants, etc.) on your own and if changes are made, Henrichsen Support will assist you at cost (re-request)?

What would you say if you did not have comprehensive process documentation from us according to the GoBS principles?

What would you say if you don't receive training videos from us?
